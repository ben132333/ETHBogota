DeMuse // How it works

DeMuse tokens give you partial ownership of a song's streaming royalty rights, plus access to exclusive content and other benefits. Ownership percentage and extras vary across each tier. DeMuse will notify collectors when royalties are available to claim after the artist gets paid.

Artists sell.

Collectors buy.
Collectors can buy royalty ownership in songs directly from their favorite artists in the form of tokens.
Both rise.
