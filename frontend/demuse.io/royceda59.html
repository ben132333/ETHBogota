About

Hailing from Detroit, Royce da 5'9" is a rapper, songwriter, and record producer known for his lyrical mastery, raw persona, and collaborations with top hip hop artists such as Eminem and Dr. Dre. Royce has built a reputation through both his solo work and his role in groups Slaughterhouse, PRhyme, and Bad Meets Evil. Since entering the scene in the 90s battling rappers at open mic nights in Detroit, he has released a full breadth of work that includes 11 mixtapes, 8 solo albums, and 6 collaboration albums.
